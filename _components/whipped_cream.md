ingredients:
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 tsp flavoring (optional) [e.g. vanilla, peppermint]

directions:
- whip cream up to medium speed
- add sugar
- add flavor if desired
- whip until stiff

---

Simple whipped cream. Best from a stand mixer.
