ingredients:
- 4 Tbls hot coffee
- 1/2 cup of butter (soft)
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 8oz cream cheese
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 tsp salt

directions:
- blend cream cheese until smooth
- blend in butter and coffee
- blend in powdered sugar and vanilla
- blend until smooth
