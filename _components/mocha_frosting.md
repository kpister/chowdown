ingredients:
- 4 Tbls hot coffee
- 4 Tbls melted butter
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 6 Tbls cocoa (unsweet)
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 tsp salt

directions:
- dry = { sift powdered sugar and cocoa and salt }
- blend butter and coffee
- blend in dry and vanilla
- blend until smooth
